Conceptmin-exp
This symbol denotes the binary minimum function which takes a set as its first argument and an expression as the second one, and returns the element in the set that minimizes the expression.
Type(sts.omdoc#sts)
(Set),Expression↦OrderedSet
Example

min-exp( 1 9 5 ,λx1x)= 9 .

min-exp( 1 9 5 ,λx1x)= 9 1 9 5 1 9
Theorem

∀S.max(S)=max-exp(S,λxx).

∀S.min(S)=min-exp(S,λxx)
